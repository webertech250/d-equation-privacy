D-Equation 20 – Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 29, 2026

D-Equation 20 (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Data Type Required / Optional Purpose
Email Required Account creation, login, and communication
Phone Number Optional User communication and account verification
Photos Optional Property listings uploaded by landlords
Messages & listings Required App functionality and user interactions
Note: We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Allow landlords and tenants to interact and manage property listings
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Retention

  • Your account data (email, phone number, listings, messages, and uploaded photos) is retained as long as your account exists.
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained for app performance monitoring.
  • Any legally required records may be retained for a limited period as required by law.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS) for all data sent between your device and our servers
  • Secure storage of user information in our database

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. All updates will be reflected on this page with the revised effective date.
